Miscellaneous Links

Links to web sites with more or less tenuous connection to elf.org.

Note that it's the totally unexpected cross-links like this that help to create the small world network that the web sites of the world form. Do your part to reduce the predictability of the web.

  • The Electronic Frontier Foundation is a frequently mistyped domain name. I get lots of e-mail that appears to be destined for eff.org, and occasionally a surfer who can't find some essential document at the wrong web site.
  • The Santa Fe Aikikai is my Aikido dojo.
  • Peace Game is an attempt to figure out ways to solve intractable conflicts.
  • Dream Tide is Caroline's web site.
  • Fiend Consulting is Talaya's web site.
  • The Madrona Institute is Merle's San Juan Islands website.
  • The Santa Fe Complex is our local effort at self-organization.
